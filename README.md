# US-Customer-Insight-EDA-Project

## Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer demographics, spending behavior, engagement patterns, and lifestyle characteristics using statistical techniques and exploratory data analysis (EDA). The objective is to uncover meaningful business insights, validate assumptions through hypothesis testing, and support data-driven decision-making for a retail company.

The analysis was performed on a synthetic customer dataset containing over 10,000 records and includes variables such as age, gender, education level, marital status, pet ownership, monthly spending, and customer interaction history.

---

## Objectives

* Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) on customer data.
* Generate descriptive statistics for key variables.
* Visualize customer demographics and spending behavior.
* Analyze relationships between customer attributes.
* Conduct hypothesis testing using statistical methods.
* Derive actionable business insights and recommendations.

## Key Findings

* Customer spending is highly skewed, with a small group of high-value customers contributing significantly to revenue.
* Gender, education level, and geographic location show minimal influence on spending behavior.
* Marital status and pet ownership exhibit a statistically significant relationship.
* Behavioral segmentation is more effective than demographic segmentation for customer targeting.
* Medium-spending customers represent a strong opportunity for future growth through personalized engagement.

---

## Statistical Tests Performed

| Business Question                           | Statistical Test    |
| ------------------------------------------- | ------------------- |
| Do males and females spend differently?     | Independent T-Test  |
| Does education level impact spending?       | One-Way ANOVA       |
| Is marital status related to pet ownership? | Chi-Square Test     |
| Are older customers less active?            | Pearson Correlation |
| Does spending vary across states?           | ANOVA               |

## Business Recommendations

* Develop loyalty programs for high-spending customers.
* Use behavioral data rather than demographic data for marketing campaigns.
* Implement upselling strategies for medium-value customers.
* Create personalized engagement campaigns based on customer activity.
* Leverage customer segmentation to improve retention and customer lifetime value.
